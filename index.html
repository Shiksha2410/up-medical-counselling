UP NEET Counselling 2024: Admission Process, Eligibility, Fees & Seat Allotment

UP NEET Counselling is the state-level counseling process conducted by the Directorate of Medical Education and Training (DMET), Uttar Pradesh for admission to MBBS, BDS, and other medical courses in government and private medical colleges in Uttar Pradesh. The process is based on the NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) score, and eligible candidates can secure admission through state quota and private quota seats.

Eligibility Criteria for UP NEET Counselling 2024

  • Nationality: Indian citizens, NRI, OCI & PIO candidates are eligible.
  • Domicile: Candidates must be residents of Uttar Pradesh or have completed their schooling in UP.
  • Educational Qualification: Must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English.
  • Minimum Marks Requirement:
    • General Category: 50% in PCB
    • OBC/SC/ST Category: 40% in PCB
    • PWD Candidates: 45% in PCB
  • NEET Qualification: Candidates must qualify for NEET 2024 as per the category-wise cutoffs.

UP NEET Counselling Process 2024

  1. Online Registration
    • Visit upneet.gov.in
    • Click on the “New Registration” link
    • Enter NEET details, personal details, and upload required documents
    • Pay the non-refundable registration fee of ₹2,000
  2. Security Deposit Payment
    • Candidates must deposit a security fee (Refundable after counselling)
    • Govt. Colleges: ₹30,000
    • Private MBBS Colleges: ₹2,00,000
    • Private BDS Colleges: ₹1,00,000
  3. Merit List Publication
    • DMET releases a merit list based on NEET scores. Only listed candidates can proceed with choice filling.
  4. Choice Filling & Locking
    • Select preferred colleges and courses online
    • Lock choices before the deadline
  5. Seat Allotment
    • Seats are allocated based on NEET Rank, Choices, Reservation & Availability
    • Candidates must download the Allotment Letter
  6. Reporting & Admission Confirmation
    • Visit the allotted college with original documents and fee payment to confirm admission
